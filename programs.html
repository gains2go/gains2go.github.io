Gains2go Full Body Ring Training Routine - Basic Level

This training routine aims at athletes of all genders and ages with little or no training experience. All muscles and all fundamental movements are trained in this routine with the goal of muscle hypertrophy, strength and pre-conditioning for more advanced ring skills. The only equipment needed are a pair of adjustable gymnastic rings.
